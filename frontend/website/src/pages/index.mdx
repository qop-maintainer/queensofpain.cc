## Who are Queens of Pain CC?

Inclusive Edinburgh cycling community. Open to all women & non-binary folks.
At 7pm every Wed, ride for 1hr, go home happy. Repeat!

We are a cycling community based in Edinburgh, Scotland that centres the
experience of women in cycling.

> All bikes and all bodies are represented, no one ever gets left behind
> and there is zero pressure to be the fastest, the most kitted out or have
> the fastest bike.
>
> _Alison D_

![Picture of Queens of Pain members before setting off on a ride.](/gallery/hero-image.webp)

Keep an eye here or on our Instagram stories to join us for one of our
rides! Once you've come along for at least one ride, we will invite you to
our WhatsApp community.
